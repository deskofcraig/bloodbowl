## Overview

Skills are learned abilities; Traits are innate characteristics. Both are defined as either **Active** or **Passive**.

- **Active**: Can be used when the player is Standing and not Distracted, unless the skill states otherwise.
- **Passive**: Always in effect regardless of player status.
- Skills/Traits marked with **\*** must always be used when applicable — they are not optional.
- Skills marked with **⚡ ELITE** (Block, Guard, Dodge, Mighty Blow) are considered Elite Skills and may be subject to limits in tournament play.

## AGILITY SKILLS

### Catch (Active)
This player may re-roll any failed AG test when attempting to Catch the ball.

### Diving Catch (Active)
This player may attempt to Catch the ball if it lands in a square in their Tackle Zone as a result of a Pass, Throw-in or Kick-off (not Bounce). Additionally, this player applies +1 to their AG test when catching in the target square of a Pass Action.

### Diving Tackle (Active)
When an opposition player attempts to leave this player's Tackle Zone via Dodge, Leap or Jump, and all modifiers and re-rolls have been applied, this player may use this Skill. Apply a −2 modifier to the opposition player's AG test and immediately place this player Prone in the square vacated. Only one player with this Skill may use it at a time against the same dodge.

### Dodge ⚡ ELITE (Active)
Once per Turn, this player may re-roll a single failed AG test when attempting to Dodge. Additionally, this Skill modifies the Stumble block result when an opposition player performs a Block Action against this player.

### Defensive (Active)
During the opposition's turns, players Marked by this player cannot use the Guard or Put the Boot In Skills.

### Hit and Run (Active)
After fully resolving a Block Action or Stab Special Action while Standing, this player may immediately move one free square ignoring Tackle Zones. After the free move, the player must not be Marking or Marked by any opposition player. A player with Hit and Run cannot also have Frenzy.

### Jump Up (Active)
This player may stand up for free without spending 3 squares of movement. Additionally, while Prone, this player may declare a Block Action: make an AG+1 test; if passed, stand and perform the Block; if failed, remain Prone and activation ends.

### Leap (Active)
During a Move Action, this player may attempt to Leap over any adjacent square regardless of content. Works like Jumping but reduces the negative modifier by 1 (minimum −1). Cannot be used alongside the Pogo Trait.

### Safe Pair of Hands (Active)
If this player would be Knocked Down, Fall Over, or placed Prone while carrying the ball, they may place the ball in any adjacent unoccupied square instead of it bouncing.

### Sidestep (Active)
Whenever this player is Pushed Back for any reason, this player's coach — not the opposing coach — chooses which unoccupied adjacent square the player moves to. Cannot be used if no unoccupied adjacent square exists.

### Sprint (Active)
This player may attempt one additional Rush beyond the normal limit when performing a Move Action.

### Sure Feet (Active)
Once per Turn, this player may re-roll a single D6 when attempting to Rush.

## DEVIOUS SKILLS

### Dirty Player (Active)
When this player performs a Foul Action, they may apply a +1 modifier to either the Armour Roll or the Injury Roll. This modifier may be applied after the roll.

### Eye Gouge (Active)
When an opposition player is Pushed Back by this player, that player cannot provide Offensive or Defensive Assists until after they are next activated.

### Fumblerooski (Active)
When this player performs a Move Action while carrying the ball, they may deliberately place the ball on the ground in any square they move out of. This does not cause a Turnover.

### Lethal Flight (Active)
When this player is thrown as part of a Throw Team-Mate Action and lands in or bounces into a square containing an opposition player who is then Knocked Down, this player may apply a +1 modifier to either the Armour Roll or Injury Roll. Modifier may be applied after the roll.

### Lone Fouler (Active)
If this player is the only player on the team yet to activate, they may perform a Foul Action without ending the team turn — provided no other player has yet acted this turn.

### Pile Driver (Active)
When this player follows up a Block that results in a Knocked Down opponent and then themselves goes Prone, they may apply a +1 modifier to the Armour Roll against the knocked-down player.

### Put the Boot In (Active)
When performing a Foul Action, this player may re-roll the Armour Roll if the initial roll fails to break armour. The re-roll result must be accepted.

### Quick Foul (Active)
This player may perform a Foul Action without being the active player, at any point during their team's turn. They cannot also have Sneaky Git.

### Saboteur (Active)
Once per game, when this player provides an assist for a Block, they may apply a −1 modifier to the blocked player's Armour Roll if the result is a POW or POW+Push.

### Shadowing (Active)
When an adjacent opposition player attempts to Dodge away, after the dodge is resolved, this player may immediately attempt a free Dodge into the square vacated. If the free Dodge fails, this player Falls Over.

### Sneaky Git (Active)
When this player performs a Foul Action and both dice of the Armour Roll show the same number, this player is not Sent Off — provided the result does not exceed 1 on either die above the minimum needed to break armour. Cannot be used with Quick Foul.

### Violent Innovator (Active)
Once per game, when this player performs a Block, they may replace one of the Block dice with a result of their choice (Push, Stumble, Both Down, or POW+Push as appropriate).

## GENERAL SKILLS

### Block ⚡ ELITE (Active)
When this player is the target of a Block Action and the result is Both Down, this player may choose to treat the result as a Push (and not go Prone). Additionally, when this player performs a Block and rolls Attacker Down, they may treat it as Both Down instead.

### Dauntless (Active)
When this player attempts to Block an opposition player with higher Strength, roll a D6 and add this player's ST. If the result equals or exceeds the opponent's ST, use the opponent's ST value for the block (treat as equal strength). If lower, use this player's own ST.

### Fend (Active)
When this player is Pushed Back as the result of a Block Action, the player performing the Block may not follow up into the square vacated.

### Frenzy\* (Active)
This player must always follow up after pushing an opponent during a Block or Blitz Action and must always perform a second block against the same opponent if they are still Standing. If this player Blitzes, they must always target the same player with the second block.

### Kick (Active)
When this player's team kicks off, this player may reposition the kick to any square in the opponent's half of the pitch, and may also reduce the Scatter rolls applied to the kicked ball.

### Pro (Active)
Once per Turn, after failing a roll, this player may roll a D6. On a 4+, they may re-roll the failed roll. A Pro roll cannot be used to re-roll another Pro roll.

### Steady Footing (Active)
This player may re-roll any failed Go For It (Rush) roll once per Rush attempt.

### Strip Ball (Active)
When this player performs a Block that results in a Push Back, the ball is automatically knocked from the carrier's hands and bounces — even if the result would not normally cause the ball to be dropped.

### Sure Hands (Active)
This player may re-roll a failed pick-up roll. Additionally, Strip Ball has no effect when used against this player.

### Tackle (Active)
Opposition players in this player's Tackle Zone may not use the Dodge skill to re-roll failed dodge rolls. Additionally, the Wrestle skill has no effect against this player.

### Taunt (Active)
Once per game, this player may declare Taunt before an opposition player's Block Action. If declared, the blocking player must target this player with the Block if possible, even if another target would be preferred.

### Wrestle (Active)
When this player is involved in a Block (as attacker or defender) and the result is Both Down, this player may choose to have both players go Prone instead of the normal Both Down result. Ignores the Tackle skill.

## MUTATION SKILLS

### Big Hand (Active)
This player ignores the modifiers from Tackle Zones when attempting to pick up or catch the ball.

### Claws (Passive)
When rolling to break armour following a block result that Knocks Down the opponent, a natural roll of 8+ on the combined 2D6 always breaks armour regardless of the opponent's AV value.

### Disturbing Presence\* (Passive)
Any player — friend or foe — attempting to pass, catch, or interfere with a pass within this player's Tackle Zone must subtract 1 from their roll.

### Extra Arms (Active)
This player may re-roll a single failed pick-up or catch roll once per action.

### Foul Appearance\* (Passive)
Any opposition player wishing to block this player must first roll a D6; on a 1, they may not perform the Block Action (but their activation does not end).

### Horns (Active)
When this player performs a Blitz Action, they gain +1 to their Strength value for the purpose of that single block.

### Iron Hard Skin (Passive)
Claws has no effect against this player. When calculating whether a Claws roll would break armour against this player, treat their AV as 1 higher.

### Monstrous Mouth (Active)
This player may re-roll a failed catch roll. Additionally, opposition players cannot use the Diving Catch skill against this player.

### Prehensile Tail (Active)
When an adjacent opposition player attempts to Dodge, Leap or Jump away from this player's Tackle Zone, apply a −1 modifier to that roll.

### Tentacles (Active)
When an adjacent opposition player attempts to Dodge, Leap or Jump away, roll a D6 after declaring the attempt; on a 4+ the opposition player is held in place and their activation ends (but they do not Fall Over).

### Two Heads (Active)
This player applies a +1 modifier to all Dodge rolls they make.

### Very Long Legs (Active)
This player applies a +1 modifier to Jump Up rolls. Additionally, opposition players cannot use other players (friend or foe) to block this player's Line of Sight.

## PASSING SKILLS

### Accurate (Active)
This player applies a +1 modifier to their PA test when making any Pass Action.

### Cannoneer (Active)
When this player makes a Long Pass or Long Bomb, the range band's negative PA modifier is reduced by 1 (e.g., Long Pass −1 becomes 0; Long Bomb −2 becomes −1).

### Cloud Burster (Active)
When making any Pass Action, this player may choose to ignore the interception attempt of one opposition player of their choice.

### Dump-Off (Active)
When this player is declared the target of a Blitz Action and has not yet acted this turn, they may immediately perform a Quick Pass before the Blitz is resolved. This free pass does not take their activation; they then act normally if the Blitz does not prevent it.

### Give and Go (Active)
After fully resolving a Pass Action (successful or not, but not fumbled), this player may immediately move up to 2 squares ignoring Tackle Zones.

### Hail Mary Pass (Active)
When this player makes a Pass Action, they may choose to ignore all range restrictions and throw to any square on the pitch. The pass is always inaccurate but is never a fumble.

### Leader (Passive) ⚡ ELITE
While this player is Standing and on the pitch, the team gains one additional Team Re-Roll each half, in addition to their normal allocation. This re-roll does not carry over between halves.

### Nerves of Steel (Active)
This player may ignore all Tackle Zone modifiers when making a Pass, Catch or Intercept roll.

### On the Ball (Active)
During the Kick-Off, after the ball has been kicked, this player may immediately move up to 3 squares.

### Pass (Active)
This player may re-roll a single failed PA test when making a Pass Action.

### Punt (Active)
When this player performs a Kick Action at kick-off, the scatter roll for the ball's distance is reduced — typically from 3D6 scatter to a more controlled single scatter roll. Check the current rulebook for precise wording.

### Safe Pass (Active)
If this player fails a PA test when making a Pass Action, the result is treated as Incomplete rather than a Fumble. No Turnover is caused unless the ball fails to leave the player's hand entirely.

## STRENGTH SKILLS

### Arm Bar (Active)
When this player assists a Block that results in a POW or POW+Push, apply a −1 modifier to the Armour Roll against the knocked-down player.

### Brawler (Active)
When this player performs a Block with only a single Block die (i.e., they are at a strength disadvantage), they may re-roll that single die once.

### Break Tackle (Active)
When this player attempts to Dodge, they may use their Strength characteristic instead of their Agility. **ST 4 or higher** provides a +2 bonus to the roll; **ST 3** provides a +1 bonus.

### Bullseye (Active) ★ NEW in BB2025
When this player performs or is involved in a Throw Team-Mate Action and the thrown player would scatter on landing, the thrown player instead lands exactly in the target square — no scatter roll is made. This is particularly powerful for targeted plays and cage-cracking TTM blitzes.

### Grab (Active)
When this player Pushes Back an opponent, they may choose any unoccupied square adjacent to both themselves and the opponent as the Push destination, rather than following the usual Push rules. Ignores Fend and Sidestep.

### Guard ⚡ ELITE (Active)
This player may provide an Offensive or Defensive Assist even if they are themselves being Marked by one or more opposition players.

### Juggernaut (Active)
When this player performs a Blitz Action, they may treat a Push or Stumble block result as a POW+Push instead. Additionally, Fend, Stand Firm and Wrestle have no effect against this player during a Blitz.

### Mighty Blow ⚡ ELITE (Active)
When this player is involved in a Block Action that results in the target being Knocked Down, this player may apply a +1 modifier to either the Armour Roll or the Injury Roll. The modifier may be applied after the roll.

### Multiple Block (Active)
When this player performs a Block Action, they may declare that they are blocking two adjacent opposition players simultaneously. Roll one Block die per opponent using this player's full ST vs each opponent's ST. Each knocked-down player makes a separate Armour Roll.

### Stand Firm (Active)
When this player would be Pushed Back as a result of a Block, they may choose not to be moved. The attacking player cannot follow up into the square the Standing Firm player occupies.

### Strong Arm (Active)
When this player performs a Throw Team-Mate Action, the range band's negative modifier is reduced by 1 (minimum 0). This makes long TTM throws significantly more reliable.

### Thick Skull (Passive)
When an Injury Roll is made against this player, apply a −1 modifier to the result. This means rolls that would be a Casualty may become a KO, and rolls that would be a KO may become a Stun.

## TRAITS

Traits are innate characteristics — they cannot be learned. All traits marked with **\*** are mandatory and must be used whenever applicable.

| Trait | Type | Notes |
|-------|------|-------|
| Always Hungry\* | Active | When this player throws a team-mate, roll a D6; on a 1, they eat the thrown player instead (player removed, no injury roll). |
| Animal Savagery\* | Passive | At the start of each activation, roll D6; on a 1 or 2, this player must attempt to Block or Blitz the nearest player (friend or foe), then their activation ends. |
| Animosity (X)\* | Active | This player may not be assisted by specific team-mates (usually of a particular position type X). Roll D6 when attempting to hand off to those players; on a 1, the hand-off fails. |
| Ball & Chain\* | Active | This player always moves in a random direction (scatter dice); they may Rush normally. On a failed Rush, they Fall Over. |
| Bloodlust (X+)\* | Passive | At the start of each activation, roll a D6; on a result below X, this player must bite an adjacent player (friend or foe). Failure to satisfy bloodlust means the player is removed from play (casualty). |
| Bombardier | Active | This player may perform a Throw Bomb Special Action instead of a Pass. If the bomb lands on a player, that player is immediately Knocked Down and an Armour Roll is made. |
| Bone Head\* | Passive | At the start of each activation, roll a D6; on a 1, this player stands still (counts as moved) and cannot perform any action this turn. |
| Breathe Fire | Active | Once per game, this player may use Breathe Fire as a Special Action: all players in a line or template take a Knocked Down result and Armour Roll is made. |
| Chainsaw\* | Active | When this player performs a Block or Foul Action, they use a Chainsaw — roll a single D6 and add to AV; on a 1, the Chainsaw kicks back and hits the player themselves. |
| Decay\* | Passive | When this player suffers a Casualty result, the Casualty is permanent — no Apothecary or Regeneration may be used. |
| Drunkard\* | Passive | At the start of each drive, roll D6; on a 1, this player is too drunk to play and must miss this drive. |
| Hatred (X)\* | Passive | This player hates players of type X. When adjacent to such players, must re-roll any Assist or Block results that don't result in the hated player being pushed or knocked down. |
| Hypnotic Gaze | Active | This player may attempt to Hypnotise an adjacent opposition player: roll D6 vs opponent's AG; if this player wins, the hypnotised player loses their Tackle Zone until the start of the next turn. |
| Insignificant\* | Passive | This player cannot be the target of a Pass or Throw Team-Mate Action by other players on their team. |
| Kick Team-Mate | Passive | A team-mate with this Trait and the Throw Team-Mate Trait may kick a Stunty team-mate as part of a Throw Team-Mate Action. |
| Loner (X+)\* | Passive | When this player wishes to use a Team Re-Roll, roll a D6; on a result lower than X, the re-roll is lost and the original result stands. |
| My Ball\* | Passive | This player will not willingly hand off the ball to any team-mate. If forced to (e.g. by Fumblerooski), the ball is immediately dropped instead. |
| No Ball\* | Passive | This player will not pick up the ball. If the ball lands in their square, it bounces away. |
| Pick-Me-Up | Active | Once per game, when a team-mate within 2 squares is Stunned, this player may immediately attempt to Prone them (standing them up instead). |
| Plague Ridden | Passive | When a player with this Trait causes a Casualty, the injured player gains the Decay trait permanently (or equivalent plague effect). |
| Pogo | Active | This player may Leap over any number of adjacent squares in a straight line during a Move Action. Cannot be used alongside the Leap Skill. |
| Projectile Vomit | Active | Once per game, this player may projectile vomit on an adjacent player: roll D6 vs their AG; if successful, the target is Knocked Down and an Armour Roll is made with Mighty Blow (+1). |
| Really Stupid\* | Passive | At the start of each activation, roll a D6; on a 1 or 2, this player stands still and cannot act unless they are adjacent to a non-Loner team-mate, in which case re-roll. |
| Regeneration | Passive | When this player suffers a KO or Casualty result, roll D6 after the roll is made; on a 4+, the player Regenerates and is placed in the Reserves box instead. |
| Right Stuff\* | Passive | This player may be thrown by a team-mate with the Throw Team-Mate Trait. |
| Secret Weapon\* | Passive | After any drive in which this player was on the pitch, they are Sent Off (regardless of whether they were ejected or not). They return after the drive, and a Bribe may be used to prevent the Send Off. |
| Stab | Active | Instead of performing a normal Block, this player may Stab — roll a D6; on a 4+ the target is Knocked Down (no block dice used). If the Stab Knocks Down the attacker instead, it is treated as the attacker Going For It too many times. |
| Stunty\* | Passive | This player applies a −1 modifier to all dodge rolls they make. They may also be the target of Throw Team-Mate. |
| Swoop | Active | When this player is thrown as part of a Throw Team-Mate Action, they may choose where they land within the scatter range rather than scattering randomly. |
| Take Root\* | Passive | At the start of each activation, roll a D6; on a 1, this player cannot move (but may still perform other actions from their current square) this turn. |
| Throw Team-Mate | Active | This player may perform a Throw Team-Mate Special Action instead of a Pass Action, throwing a team-mate with the Right Stuff trait. |
| Timmm-ber! | Passive | When this player Falls Over (from Take Root or other effects), place them Prone without making an Armour Roll — they simply topple over. |
| Titchy\* | Passive | This player provides no Tackle Zone modifier. Players do not have to dodge to leave this player's tackle zone (they have no TZ for dodge purposes). However, this player still counts as marking for Gang Foul purposes. |
| Trickster | Active | Once per game, after being Pushed Back, this player may swap positions with the pushing player (both end up where the other started). |
| Unchannelled Fury\* | Passive | At the start of each activation, roll D6; on a 1, this player is overcome with rage and must Blitz the nearest player — friend or foe — then their activation ends. |
| Unsteady\* | Passive | At the end of this player's activation, if they have moved at all, roll D6; on a 1 they Fall Over. |

## Elite Skills Summary (Tournament Reference)

The following four skills are designated **Elite Skills** in BB2025. Tournament rules often cap how many of each can appear on a single roster or per player.

| Elite Skill | Category | Why It's Elite |
|-------------|----------|----------------|
| **Block** | General | Fundamental survival and blocking power |
| **Guard** | Strength | Provides assists even when marked — network effect |
| **Dodge** | Agility | Fundamental evasion and survival |
| **Mighty Blow (+1)** | Strength | +1 to armour/injury = dramatically increased casualty rate |
