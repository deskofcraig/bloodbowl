# Blood Bowl 2025 – All Star Players Reference
> Sources: bloodbowlbase.ru/bb2025/starplayers/ · bbdugout.com · NAF Regulations 2026  
> ★ = Elite Skill · Stats verified where possible; verify all values against the latest FAQ.  
> All star players have **Loner (4+)** unless otherwise stated. Cost = gold pieces (gp).

---

## HOW STAR PLAYERS WORK

- **Requires 11 regular players** before any star player may be hired.
- Stars are available to teams based on **league affiliation** (see each entry).
- **Paired stars** (Grak & Crumbleberry, Dribl & Drull, Lucian & Valen Swift) must be hired **together** as a pair; they occupy two player slots and their combined cost covers both.
- Stars cannot receive additional skills or characteristic upgrades.
- A star player hired as an **inducement** only plays for that one match.

**League Abbreviations:**
`OWC` Old World Classic · `EKL` Elven Kingdoms League · `BB` Badlands Brawl  
`CC` Chaos Clash · `WES` Worlds Edge Superleague · `LS` Lustrian Superleague  
`UC` Underworld Challenge · `SL` Sylvan League
